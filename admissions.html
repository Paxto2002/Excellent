Admission Procedure

  • Parents must acknowledge and be satisfied with the school's curriculum, vision, mission, teaching techniques, rules & regulations, activities, and fee structure.
  • Students must meet the mentioned age group criteria.
  • Registration and admission test are mandatory.
  • Parent's interview with the admin, principal, and section head is necessary.
  • Read & sign the Terms & Conditions and follow them carefully.
  • Submit all required documents and the full fee voucher.
